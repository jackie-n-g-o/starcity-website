Life at Starcity

We Share

We love our weekly dinners. Ideas, food, and space are shared to elevate our dedication to sustainable living. We opt-in to community events when we feel comfortable to do so.

We Love

Hassle-free living and a diversity of perspectives! Starcity takes care of our home - we don’t worry about weekly cleaning, basic amenities, package deliveries and more. The members of our home come from different parts of the world, have different backgrounds and professions, and healthy perspectives on how to live collectively.

We Participate

While we’re busy people, Starcity helps us give back easily by arranging volunteer opportunities with local non-profits and suggesting local small-business owners that we can support.
